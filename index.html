Mexico City Adventure

Our Family Trip: Hot Air Balloons, Pyramids, Tacos and So Much Ice Cream!

Mon 11 Aug - Arrival

We arrived in Mexico City and made our way to our Airbnb in Condesa!

Tue 12 Aug - Condesa Day

Avocado toast, churro-making class, street market, dinner at MeroToro.

Wed 13 Aug - Hot Air Balloon and Pyramids

Hot air balloon over Teotihuacan, La Gruta cave restaurant, climbing pyramids.

Thu 14 Aug - Templo Mayor

Panaderia, Templo Mayor skull wall, Zocalo.

Fri 15 Aug - Casa Azul and Coyoacan

Casa Azul (Frida Kahlo), ice cream in Coyoacan, coyote fountain.

Sat 16 Aug - Xochimilco and Anthropology Museum

Xochimilco flower crowns, Anthropology Museum, ice cream.

Sun 17 Aug - Sunday Funday

Street barbacoa tacos, Panaderia Rosetta, watching Muevete en Bici.

Mon 18 Aug - Heading Home

An incredible week of balloons, pyramids, tacos, and ice cream!
